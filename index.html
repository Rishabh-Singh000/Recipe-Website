Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya
  • Milk
  • Maida
  • Oil/Ghee
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water, add cardamom & rose water. Keep syrup warm.
  2. Mix khoya, maida, baking soda, and milk to make a soft dough.
  3. Shape small smooth balls.
  4. Fry on low heat until golden brown.
  5. Soak hot jamuns in warm syrup for 1-2 hours.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder (do not over-grind).
  2. Heat sugar and water to make a one-string syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder and cardamom, cook while stirring.
  4. When it forms a soft dough, remove from heat.
  5. Roll thin, cut into diamond shapes.
